Healing Touch

What is Healing Touch?

Healing Touch is a relaxing, nurturing energy therapy that uses gentle, intentional touch on or near the body to support balance and well-being.

A complementary practice

Gentle care centered on comfort.

Healing Touch is a heart-centered practice in which the practitioner uses light touch or works just above the body. The intention is to create a calm, supportive experience and encourage relaxation and a sense of balance.

It may be used on its own as a wellness practice or alongside conventional health care. Healing Touch does not replace medical evaluation, diagnosis, medication, psychotherapy, or other treatment recommended by a licensed health professional.

Why people may choose Healing Touch

  • To make space for deep relaxation
  • For comfort during stressful seasons
  • As part of a personal wellness routine
  • For quiet emotional or spiritual reflection
  • Alongside standard medical care
  • To feel supported and cared for
Important: No specific result can be guaranteed. Experiences vary from person to person, and any health concern should be discussed with an appropriate licensed health professional.

What happens during a session?

A calm experience from beginning to end.

A typical session includes a brief conversation before and after the hands-on portion. You remain fully clothed and may sit or lie down in a comfortable position.

During Healing Touch, Anne may place her hands gently in a series of positions or work just above the body. Some people report feeling warmth, tingling, lightness, or deep relaxation; others simply experience quiet rest. There is no “right” sensation to have.

Music or aromatherapy may be included based on your preferences. Please share allergies, scent sensitivities, mobility needs, touch preferences, or other comfort concerns before the session.

Common questions

What you may want to know.

Do I stay fully clothed?

Yes. Healing Touch sessions are provided while you remain fully clothed.

Do I have to be touched?

No. Techniques can be performed with hands just above the body. Your preferences and consent guide the session at all times.

Is Healing Touch medical treatment?

No. It is a complementary wellness practice and should not be used in place of medical or mental health care.

What might I feel?

Experiences vary. You may notice relaxation, warmth, tingling, lightness, or no particular sensation. All of those experiences are normal.

How long is a session?

A typical session is approximately 60 minutes, including a brief conversation before and after the treatment portion. Call or text Anne to confirm the current session format.

Can I request no fragrance?

Yes. Aromatherapy is optional. Please mention allergies or scent sensitivities before your appointment.
