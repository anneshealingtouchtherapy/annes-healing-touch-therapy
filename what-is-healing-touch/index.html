What is Healing Touch?

Healing Touch is an International Board Certified Energy Based Therapy.

The gentle, self-healing promoting techniques can be used exclusively or integrated with traditional medical care in treating conditions such as but not limited to:

  • Acute and Chronic Pain
  • Depression and Anxiety
  • Sleep Disorders
  • Pre and Post-Surgery
  • Hospice Support
  • Acute and Chronic Illness
  • Emotional and Spiritual Challenges
  • Strengthen Immune System

What happens in a treatment session?

  • A session typically lasts 60 minutes (includes verbal exchange before and after treatment)
  • You will sit or lie down in a comfortable position with your clothes on
  • Music and Aromatherapy may be added to your session
  • You may feel air moving around you and hands placed on you gently in different positions
  • You may feel a deep relaxation, heat or tingling sensations
