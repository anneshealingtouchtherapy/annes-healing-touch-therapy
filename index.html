Healing Touch

A relaxing energy therapy that uses gentle, intentional touch on or just above the body. It is offered as a complementary practice to encourage relaxation, comfort, and a sense of balance.

Learn what to expect

Reflexology

Focused pressure is applied to reflex areas, commonly on the feet, in a calm and comfortable setting. Many people choose reflexology as part of a personal relaxation and wellness routine.

Your session

Simple, comfortable, and unhurried.

Begin with a conversation

Anne will listen to what brings you in, answer questions, and discuss comfort preferences before the session begins.

Settle in comfortably

You remain fully clothed and may sit or lie down. Quiet music or aromatherapy may be included only when desired.

Close with reflection

There is time afterward to notice how you feel, ask questions, and transition gently back into your day.
